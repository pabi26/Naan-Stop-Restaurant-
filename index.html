Our Story

At Spice Garden, we bring you the authentic flavors and aromas of the Indian subcontinent to delight your taste buds in the heart of downtown Toronto.

We also have locations in Burnaby, British Columbia and Montreal, Quebec, where we offer the same fine dining experience and mouth-watering Indian cuisine.

Our restaurant offers a fine dining experience where you can indulge in the rich and diverse flavors of both North and South Indian cuisine.

Our menu is carefully crafted to cater to a wide range of tastes and dietary requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Our dishes are made using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, ensuring that every bite is a burst of authentic Indian flavors.

Come visit us at Spice Garden for a memorable dining experience that will transport you to the vibrant streets of India.
